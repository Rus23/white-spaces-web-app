Cookies

Just like most websites, our portal uses ‘cookies’. Cookies are small text files that your internet browser stores on your computer to help identify future visits on websites. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you and they can be read only by the webserver which issued the cookie.

The use of cookies is a standard procedure online and most of the browsers have been set to automatically accept all of the cookies. However, you can always decide to stop using them. You can manually delete the cookies or adjust the browser settings to operate with cookies.

Our website uses cookies only to provide you with a higher quality interaction with the portal, to statistically analyze the website traffic, and optimize our digital marketing by using Google Analytics and Google Ads.

Website analysis with Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics on our website, the service for web analysis Google Inc. Google Analytics uses cookies that are stored on your computer and that enable the analysis of the website use. The data generated by the cookie about your activity on the website is usually sent to a Google server in Europe (or a member state of the European economic area) to make the IP address anonymous and exclude the personal reference. After the IP address has been made anonymous, the reduced address is transferred and stored on the Google server in the USA. The IP address will be sent directly to the Google server in the USA and reduced there, only in extreme circumstances. This website uses Google Analytics with the extension to anonymously gather IP addresses (the so-called IP masking). On the behalf of our company, Google will use the collected data to examine your use of the website, make reports about the website activity and offer us other various services connected to the use of the website

The IP address that your browser is transferring in the context of Google Analytics is not combined with other Google data. You can opt out of the use of cookies by setting the right parameters in your browser.

However, we would like to emphasize that in this case, you won’t be able to use all of the services on our website. Furthermore, you can prevent Google from collecting and editing your data generated by cookies that have to do with the website use (including the IP address) by downloading and installing the extension from the link: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en . When you visit this website, a cookie for preventing the data collection becomes active. For more information on data protection in Google Analytics visit: https://www.google.com/analytics/terms/gb.html and https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245?hl=en
